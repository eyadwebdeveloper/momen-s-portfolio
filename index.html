📱 Social Media · Mental Health
Kaggle Dataset
// Student Behavioral Analysis
Social Media Addiction & Academic Impact

A comprehensive behavioral study analyzing social media consumption patterns across high school, undergraduate, and graduate students. The project correlates daily screen time and platform dependency to measurable mental health outcomes, sleep quality, academic performance, and the frequency of social-media-triggered life issues.

Undergraduates showed the highest average daily usage (6.4 hrs), correlating with a 23% decline in mental health scores.
Strong negative correlation (r = -0.71) between screen time and sleep duration, especially in students aged 18–22.
Students in the "high usage" group had a 41% higher incidence of anxiety-related issues compared to low-usage peers.
Graduate students displayed resilience patterns — higher usage but relatively stable academic performance due to structured habits.

⛷️ Ski Resorts · Tourism
Maven Dataset
// Tourism & Hospitality Intelligence
Ski Resort Performance Analysis

An in-depth exploration of ski resort infrastructure and its impact on guest ratings and booking volumes. By correlating facilities — gondola lifts, chair lifts, snow cannon coverage, slope variety, terrain difficulty, and seasonal snow depth — the analysis identifies the key drivers of resort success across European and North American markets.

Resorts with 10+ gondola lifts averaged a 4.6/5 guest rating vs 3.9/5 for those with fewer — a decisive infrastructure effect.
Snow cannon coverage above 70% showed a 35% uplift in off-season bookings, minimizing weather dependency.
Slope diversity (beginner + expert ratio) was the top predictor of family booking rates, outweighing total resort size.
A composite "facility score" model explained 78% of variance in peak-season booking volumes across 180+ resorts.
